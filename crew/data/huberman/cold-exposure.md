# Cold Exposure

**Source:** Huberman Lab cold-exposure / dopamine episodes.

**Why:** Brief cold (2-5 minutes, cold enough to be uncomfortable but safe) acutely raises norepinephrine ~2x and dopamine ~2.5x, sustained for hours. Improves mood, focus, and over weeks may improve metabolic markers.

**Protocol:**
- Cold shower or plunge, 2-3 minutes, daily or every other day.
- Water cold enough to want to leave; "uncomfortable but safe".
- AVOID immediately post-strength-training (blunts hypertrophy adaptation).

**Why it matters for the Captain:** Mood lift, focus lift, and a clean dopaminergic kick that competes with the scrolling habit.
