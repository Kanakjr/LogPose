# NSDR (Non-Sleep Deep Rest)

**Source:** Huberman Lab NSDR / yoga nidra episodes.

**Why:** A 10-30 minute guided body-scan / parasympathetic-down-regulation protocol that restores dopamine in basal-ganglia circuits and can substitute for ~half of a missed night's sleep on focus tasks. Different from a nap because there's no actual sleep stage gating.

**Protocol:**
- Lie flat, eyes closed, phone face-down.
- 10-20 minutes, guided audio recommended (Reveri, Huberman's own NSDR on YouTube).
- Best mid-afternoon (1-3 PM) when alertness dips.

**Why it matters for the Captain:** Recovers focus without a full nap and restores dopamine for the rest of the workday. Use after poor-sleep nights.

**LogPose voyage:** `Thousand Sunny Rest` (Conqueror's Haki, timer mode).
