# Zone 2 Cardio

**Source:** Huberman Lab fitness episodes (with Andy Galpin, Peter Attia).

**Why:** Zone 2 (~60-70% max HR, "talking pace") expands mitochondrial density, improves fat-as-fuel utilization, raises HDL, lowers triglycerides and fasting glucose. The single highest-return cardio for desk-bound adults.

**Protocol:**
- 150-180 minutes per week, in 3-5 sessions of 30-60 min.
- Heart rate: conversational - you can talk in full sentences, barely.
- Bike, brisk walk, easy run, rower, elliptical. Anything steady-state.

**Why it matters for the Captain:** Directly targets the high triglycerides, high LDL, low HDL, prediabetic HbA1c pattern flagged in the Oct 2025 labs.
