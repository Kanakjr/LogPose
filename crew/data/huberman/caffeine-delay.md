# Caffeine Delay (90-120 min Rule)

**Source:** Huberman Lab caffeine + sleep episodes.

**Why:** Adenosine builds during sleep but isn't fully cleared on waking. Caffeine within the first ~90 minutes binds adenosine receptors and creates an early-afternoon crash + delayed daytime sleepiness.

**Protocol:**
- Wait at least 90 minutes after waking before first caffeine. 120 if you can.
- Water + electrolytes first (the Sea Water Ration voyage).
- Last caffeine 8-10 hours before bed.

**Why it matters for the Captain:** Avoids the 2-4 PM crash, sharpens the natural cortisol pulse, and protects sleep onset.
